About the Initiative

This initiative aims to identify 'prevention deserts', communities at highest risk for chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity, and implement targeted early-intervention programs that expand access to preventive care.

Chronic diseases account for over 90% of U.S. healthcare spending and cause millions of preventable deaths each year. Our goal is to reverse this trend through community-based prevention and policy coordination.
